## About

Royal Access is a free WordPress plugin that adds a floating accessibility toolbar to your site, giving visitors with disabilities the tools they need to browse comfortably. With 14+ built-in features and 4 automatic fixes that run behind the scenes, Royal Access helps your site move toward WCAG compliance without requiring a complete redesign.

The toolbar floats unobtrusively on your page and lets visitors customize their experience — adjusting text size, switching to high contrast mode, enabling a dyslexia-friendly font, or highlighting links for easier navigation. These aren't cosmetic tweaks; they make a real difference for users with visual impairments, cognitive disabilities, or motor limitations who rely on keyboard navigation.

Beyond the visitor-facing toolbar, Royal Access automatically applies fixes to common accessibility issues on your site. It enhances focus indicators, improves screen reader compatibility, and ensures interactive elements are keyboard-accessible. The plugin is lightweight by design, adding minimal load time while making your site usable by a much wider audience.
